**Bratislava, 24 November 2025** – The DREAMER consortium is delighted to announce a significant expansion of its network with the addition of the [**Faculty of Management (FM) at Comenius University Bratislava (FM CU)**](https://fm.uniba.sk/en/) as an official Associated Partner.

The Comenius University Faculty of Management, a respected institution in the project's coordinating country, has formally expressed its commitment to the **DREAMER – Development and Reconstruction Empowerment through Advanced Project Management Education and Research** project.

## A Boost for Project Dissemination and Quality

The expertise and institutional reach of FM CU will be invaluable to the success of the DREAMER project, which is focused on equipping future leaders with cutting-edge project management skills for green and digital transformation, particularly in the context of Ukraine's reconstruction.

The Faculty's primary contributions as an Associated Partner will focus on two key areas:

1. **Dissemination Activities:** The FM CU team will actively support the promotion and visibility of the project's innovative outcomes—including new e-learning modules and training guides—through their academic events, communication channels, and institutional networks at the national and international levels.

2. **Piloting and Feedback:** The Faculty has committed to participating in the piloting phase of selected educational tools and training materials developed by the consortium. This crucial step will involve providing expert academic feedback and engaging both students and faculty in the testing process, ensuring the highest quality and relevance of our educational products.

This commitment, formalised in a Letter of Intent signed by the **Dean, Prof. Mgr. Peter Štarchoň, PhD.**, marks a powerful local and international endorsement for the project's mission.

The entire DREAMER consortium extends a warm welcome to our new Associated Partner. This collaboration strengthens our ability to address the highly relevant challenges in modern project management education and to make a profound impact through international cooperation.
