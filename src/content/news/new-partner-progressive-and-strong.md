We are proud to welcome [Progressive&Strong](https://progresylni.org/en), a dynamic Ukrainian non-governmental organisation (NGO), as an Associated Partner to the DREAMER project. This partnership is a key step in ensuring our project's visibility and impact, with a strong focus on dissemination.

As an associated partner, Progressive&Strong will contribute specifically to Work Package 4: Dissemination, Integration, and International Collaboration. In this
role, we will support the project by:

- Promoting DREAMER outcomes through our platforms and networks in Ukraine
and internationally,
- Engaging with relevant stakeholders, including civil society actors and
educational institutions,
- Supporting the integration of project results into local initiatives aligned with
post-war reconstruction, digitalization, and green transformation,
- Facilitating international collaboration and visibility of Ukrainian perspectives
in the project.

Their commitment will ensure that the project’s outputs—new curricula and training materials—are effectively promoted and made accessible to local communities, aligning with post-war reconstruction, digitalization, and green transformation initiatives.

The formal intent to participate, signed by **Bohdan Ferens, Founder and Chairman of the Coordination Council**, confirms Progressive&Strong's readiness to drive the project's success through robust outreach.
