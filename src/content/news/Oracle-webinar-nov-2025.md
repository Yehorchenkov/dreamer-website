On 27 November 2025, we had the pleasure to present the Erasmus+ DREAMER project (Development and Reconstruction Empowerment through Advanced Project Management Education and Research) during the webinar “Oracle Academy presents the DREAMER Project” on the Oracle Academy portal.
During the webinar, we presented how DREAMER:

- Develops advanced Project Management courses (32 ECTS) for construction, engineering and business students
- Integrates Oracle Primavera to build practical skills for managing complex projects
- Focuses on preparing students to contribute to post-war reconstruction and sustainable development in Ukraine
- Embeds digital and green competencies into teaching and learning
