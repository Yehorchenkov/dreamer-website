The DREAMER consortium is delighted to announce that the **PMI Slovakia Chapter** has officially joined the project as an Associated Partner, lending its professional expertise to our mission.

As a non-profit professional association and part of the **Project Management Institute, Inc. (PMI)**, **PMI Slovakia** represents the local extension of the global authority in project management. Its support for the DREAMER project (Development and Reconstruction Empowerment through Advanced Project Management Education and Research) is a crucial validation of our curriculum development.

The collaboration with PMI Slovakia will be vital in ensuring that the new advanced project management courses align with the highest professional standards, thereby promoting the application, understanding, and recognition of the project management profession in Slovakia and across Europe.

We extend our sincere thanks to **Ing. Peter Mikolas, PMI Slovakia Chapter President**, for confirming their commitment to this important endeavour.
