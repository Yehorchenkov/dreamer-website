The DREAMER project is pleased to announce PMI Ukraine Chapter as a new associated partner. In its official letter of support dated 24 March 2026, PMI Ukraine Chapter confirmed its commitment to supporting the project Development and Reconstruction Empowerment through Advanced Project Management Education and Research (DREAMER).

As part of this cooperation, PMI Ukraine Chapter expressed its willingness to contribute to the dissemination of project activities and results through its professional network, provide expert feedback on selected educational and training materials, promote dialogue between academia and project management practitioners, and support the visibility and practical relevance of DREAMER outputs within the wider professional community.

This new partnership represents an important step in strengthening the project’s links with the professional project management community and enhancing the practical impact of DREAMER in the context of development, recovery, and reconstruction. The consortium highly appreciates the support of PMI Ukraine Chapter and looks forward to fruitful cooperation.
