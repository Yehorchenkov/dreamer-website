Oracle Academy has published a new blog article highlighting the DREAMER project and its contribution to advancing project management education through international collaboration. Published on **17 March 2026** and authored by **Danny Gooris, Regional Director of Oracle Academy, EMEA**, the article presents DREAMER as an EU-funded initiative led by the **Slovak University of Technology in Bratislava** to support Ukraine’s recovery and strengthen project management education across Europe.

The Oracle Academy blog emphasizes that DREAMER brings together universities from **six countries** and focuses on developing **open-access, bilingual learning materials** in project management, enriched with digital tools and real-world case studies. It also highlights the project’s collaborative approach, including hands-on learning with **Oracle Primavera**, knowledge exchange among partners, and its broader mission to prepare students, teachers, and professionals for reconstruction and sustainable development challenges.

This recognition by Oracle Academy is an important milestone for the DREAMER consortium and a valuable contribution to the project’s international visibility. It reflects the relevance of DREAMER’s mission and confirms the importance of cross-border cooperation in building modern, accessible, and practice-oriented project management education.
